Podcasts I listen to

Sun, Mar 5, 2017

Last updated: Mon, Dec. 23, 2019

A podcast, as an information medium, is more popular than ever before. Soon, it seems, even my dog will have its own show. There are many good podcasts out there. Down below you’ll find ones which I listen to. Podcasts are grouped by languages, each has a label:

  • #must - I listen to every episode
  • #selectively - depending on whether I find the topic interesting or not
  • #optionally - depending on the mood

I also record podcasts, which I’ve tried to listen, but did not found particularly interesting.
