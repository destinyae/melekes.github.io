Monthly Art #25

Sun, Dec 3, 2017

This is a monthly “newsletter”, containing a list of what I enjoyed the most last month. Whether it was a full length movie, short film, music album, book or even a painting.

What I am watching

  • IMDB
  • Metacritic
  • Kinopoisk.ru

This is a story about Jimmy Iovine and Dr. Dre, cofounders of Beats Electronics (formerly known as Beats by Dr. Dre). Where did they grow up? What did it take to come to such great success?

  • Chamath Palihapitiya, Founder and CEO Social Capital, on Money as an Instrument of Change

Chamath Palihapitiya is a true capitalist. His view on money in completely different from what many of us have. Highly recommend this talk, which is full of wisdom and ideas.

What I am reading

  • At Hack the Earth, the decadence lab from the eco-colony of Calafou “Calafou” is an experiment of collective coexistence. He strives to find new ways to achieve life balance outside the capitalist system, built on the principle of economic and political self-determination.

  • The Coming Age of Killer Machines Flying killer drones with AI and facial recognition? Will this be our future? Will we be wearing Anti-surveillance clothing?

  • Голубь, лаборант, Ницше и проблема мышления “Проще сказать, что мы не в состоянии даже помыслить всю сложность последствий наших действий.” И это нормально.

  • Проблемы больших компаний “6 месяцев назад положил свой значок и пистолет бейджик на стол и ушёл из Яндекса.” Разложил по полочкам все.
