Sat, Dec 12, 2015

This is a monthly “newsletter”, containing a list of what I enjoyed the most last month. Whether it was a full length movie, short film, music album or book or even painting.

Film - Dances with Wolves (1990)

At his recent interview with Tim Ferriss, Kevin Costner said that “Dances with Wolves” is the most beloved movie of his. And I can’t agree more - it is stunning! Despite the fact that this film is 3 hours long, I never got bored or anything like that.
